MoonPay can onboard entities and individuals from regions not sanctioned by OFAC, using a risk-tiering approach. Higher-risk tiers are onboarded on a case-by-case basis depending on the risk profile.

All onboarded customers can get access to Virtual Accounts (GBP / EUR / USD), while Payin / Payout capabilities in other currencies may be limited based on jurisdiction.

# Risk Scoring Methodology

**Prohibited**: Business relationships with individuals or entities from these countries are **not permitted** under any circumstances due to strict international sanctions and high compliance risks.

**Restricted**: Customers from these countries are generally not accepted. However, in rare cases where a strong business justification exists, onboarding may be possible with executive approval and rigorous enhanced due diligence (EDD).

**High Risk**: Customers from high-risk countries require thorough scrutiny, including enhanced due diligence and approval from our Money Laundering Reporting Officer (MLRO) before onboarding.

**Medium Risk**: Customers from medium-risk countries undergo standard due diligence. However, additional verification and enhanced due diligence may apply if specific risk factors are identified.

**Low Risk**: Customers from low-risk countries go through standard due diligence procedures, ensuring a smooth onboarding process.Payin, Payout
