MoonPay is the leading ramps provider in the space and continues to be the most popular gateway for crypto and NFT applications across the world. With the MoonPay Top-up users can seamlessly Top-up their crypto wallets. Users can choose from multiple funding sources:

- MoonPay's intuitive fiat-to-crypto widget
- MoonPay Balance
- 65 local payment methods via Onramper
- Coinbase
- Direct wallet-to-wallet transfers via Swaps.xyz

The embeddable component can be integrated directly in your website or web3 application.

## Features

**Secure crypto transactions**: Our fiat-to-crypto widget supports 100+ cryptocurrencies and as the merchant of record MoonPay assumes full responsibility for all fraud disputes and chargebacks. All customers are required to pass MoonPay’s established Know Your Customer (KYC) and risk management procedures along with all applicable laws and regulations.

**Optimal conversion rates**: Millions of users have previously purchased cryptocurrencies using MoonPay, having stored their payment and KYC details, leading to maximized conversion rates.

**Diverse payment methods**: MoonPay accommodates a vast spectrum of payment methods for purchase and sale transactions: credit and debit cards, EUR / GBP / USD bank transfers, local payment methods like PIX and Yellow Card, as well as Apple & Google Pay, ensuring users can transact according to their preference. Top-up widget also support a variety of [Onramper supported payment methods](https://docs-archive.onramper.com/docs/coverage-payment) , centralized exchanges and direct wallet-to-wallet transfers.

1. Get your MoonPay account and API keys by creating an account [here](https://dashboard.moonpay.com/).
2. Immediately after creating your account, you can start using our sandbox environment.
3. Once you've completed the KYB process, your API keys will be made available in the developers tab of the dashboard.
